# RFM Segmentation — Business Insights

## Define problem

We have a significant churn problem. A quarter of our entire customer base has stopped buying and hasn't spent much.

The Champion segment is only 4%. This is acceptable because the condition for this segment is strict. But we still can raise the rate

9% revenue comes from 13% new customers, this is good. And we should take action with this opportunity

The VIP and Loyal segment is the most stable here. With decent values of customers and contribute a lot to the total revenue

Potential Loyalist is the high chance segment to develop, currently the revenue is not suprise yet but we can increase it

Big Spender is an interesting case when the total order is small, but they contribute 16% on total revenue, they can spent heavily each times, but they are not loyal

---

## Solution

### The Lost segment:

Sending mails to have a recall on customer in case they forget

Exclusive voucher for customers havent buy in >3 month

Have another project for this segment (immediately)

* When they stop ordering?

* What happens at that time? (price rising, product update, etc...)

* What is their 3 last order information? (product, customer service, etc...)

* Why they stop ordering?

* And solution

*This is really improtant because its shown our business is going down*

---

### Champion segment:

The most important is keeping them is this segment as long as we can.

Give them super voucher depending on their contribution, exclusive power (VIP members, prioritize, etc...) and have the most take care on the customer services (thank you mail, keep contact on social media, if they are big cooperation we can considering at helping their business in marketing or something relate and acceptable).

Pay attention to their reviews, comment to take immediate action

---

### New Customer segment:

They are important right now because its shown our business is not dying yet

Keep an eyes on what they buy, raising the marketing and most

Important: give extra voucher for new register account. We can accept the risk of system abuse where existing customer create multiple account to get more voucher (or have some action when 1 address have 3 or more account) since the benefit of getting new customers is higher. And the Lost segment is completely dooming right now.

---

### VIP and Loyal:

Since they are the most stable customer of our business, the best we can do is keeping them right here, and raising them to higher segment like Champion if we have chance

Create some even point directly to this segment (the more you buy, the more you get; free shipping; etc...)

Sending thank you voucher and have something make them feel important (diamond, gold, silver member...)

---

### Potential Loyalist:

This segment can revive our business

We need to move them to Loyal or even VIP segment

Make they buy more expensive products: upselling, cross-selling, bundle,...

Make they buy more products: buy 3 get 1, more voucher if they add 1 more product, etc...

Set the free shipping more than their average orders (if the average are 20$, notice them that with order more than 30$ we will free ships). This action only work when we havent have the free shipping before or else we will recieve negative reaction from most customer

Keep notice them about the benefit of sliver, gold,... member (you only ..$ aways to becoming a ... member)

---

### Big Spender segment:

This segment contain the high-valued customer. Who can easily spent hard each time visited

The point is making them more interest to raise the frequency

Since they are high-value, we can skip the discount, etc and focusing to the customer services or the conspicuous consumption
